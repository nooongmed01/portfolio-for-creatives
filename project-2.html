GON:Woman's Tae Kwon Do Studio

As we grow to become young women, we face constant pressure. To be perfect, pretty and nice. Too often it can make us feel insecure, vulnerable and unsure where we stand. GON(곤) means ‘ground’. GON Taekwondo is where women become grounded. Where women grow strong in body, mind and soul.

For this project, I aimed to empower women so that every woman can live more enterprising and boundless life by finding their potential power.
