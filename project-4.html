Milook

Milook is a cute doodle-style interacting milk package that gives the consumer happiness and motivation every morning. On the milk carton side, I made an interactive section where people can draw their feelings for every day and week so that they can feel knowing their mental state or can set how their day/week will go. On the other side, there is Milook’s little letter to the customers to emphasize how healthy this milk is, and how we are insensitive to our mental states these days.
